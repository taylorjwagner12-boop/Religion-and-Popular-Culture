What is This About?

Throughout this course, we've explored how religion intersects with popular culture—from blockbuster films to chart-topping music, from celebrity personas to viral trends. Religion isn't confined to places of worship; it's woven into the stories we watch, the music we listen to, and the figures we follow.

This project examines specific examples of religion in popular culture, analyzing what these intersections reveal about society, belief, morality, and human meaning-making.
