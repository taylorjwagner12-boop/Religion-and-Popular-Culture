Religion in Popular Culture

Exploring how religious themes, symbols, and beliefs shape modern entertainment, music, and celebrity culture

What is This About?

Throughout this course, we've explored how religion intersects with popular culture—from blockbuster films to chart-topping music, from celebrity personas to viral trends. Religion isn't confined to places of worship; it's woven into the stories we watch, the music we listen to, and the figures we follow.

This project examines specific examples of religion in popular culture, analyzing what these intersections reveal about society, belief, morality, and human meaning-making.

Key Topics Explored

📽️ Religion in Film

How movies like "I Am Legend" use religious symbolism, sacrifice, and messianic themes to explore deeper questions about faith and redemption.

🎵 Religion in Music & Artists

How musicians and celebrities draw on religious imagery, spirituality, and moral questions in their work and public personas.

🔍 Cultural Analysis

What these examples tell us about how modern audiences engage with religious ideas through entertainment.
